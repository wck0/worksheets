Activity 2.4.1.

Let \(r\) be any real number, but \(r \neq 1\text{.}\)

(a)

Show that \(\displaystyle r^k = \Delta \left[\frac{r^{k+1}}{r-1}\right]\text{.}\)

(b)

Let \(b\) be any real number. Compute \(\displaystyle\sum_{k=0}^n b r^k\text{.}\)

(c)

Compute \(\displaystyle\sum_{k=0}^{10} 3 \left(\frac{4}{5}\right)^k\text{.}\)

(d)

Compute \(\displaystyle\sum_{k=0}^8 \left(-\frac{5}{4}\right)^k\text{.}\)
