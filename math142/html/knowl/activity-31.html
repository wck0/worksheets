Activity 6.2.1.
Determine each of the antiderivatives below.
(a)
\(\displaystyle \int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{4-x^2}}\)
(b)
\(\displaystyle \int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{4-9x^2}}\)
(c)
\(\displaystyle \int \frac{dx}{4+x^2}\)
(d)
\(\displaystyle \int \frac{dx}{4+9x^2}\)
(e)
\(\displaystyle \int \frac{e^x}{1 + e^{2x}}\;dx\)
(f)
\(\displaystyle \int \frac{\tan(x)}{\sqrt{9-\cos^2(x)}}\;dx\)
