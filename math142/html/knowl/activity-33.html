Activity 6.2.3.
Verify the following antiderivative formulas by taking an appropriate derivative. In each, \(a\) is a constant.
(a)
\(\displaystyle \int \sqrt{a^2-x^2}\;dx = \frac{x}{2}\sqrt{a^2-x^2} + \frac{a^2}{2}\arcsin\left(\frac{x}{a}\right) + C\)
(b)
\(\displaystyle \int \frac{x^2}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}}\;dx = -\frac{x}{2}\sqrt{a^2-x^2} + \frac{a^2}{2}\arcsin\left(\frac{x}{a}\right) + C\)
