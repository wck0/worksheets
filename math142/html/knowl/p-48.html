Recall that the definite integral of \(f\) on the interval \([a,b]\) is defined to be

\begin{equation*} \int_a^b f(x) dx = \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{i=1}^n f(x_i)\Delta x \end{equation*}

where \(\displaystyle \Delta x = \frac{b-a}{n}\) and \(x_i = a + i \Delta x\) for \(i = 1, \dots, n\text{.}\) This definite integral represents the area under the curve \(f\) from \(a\) to \(b\text{.}\)
