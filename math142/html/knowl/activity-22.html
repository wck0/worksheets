Activity 3.2.4.

Suppose that \(\displaystyle \int_0^3 f(x)\;dx = 0\text{.}\)

(a)

Explain what this means in terms of the area under \(f\) from \(0\) to \(3\text{.}\)

(b)

Draw at least three different graphs of functions where \(\displaystyle \int_0^3 f(x)\;dx = 0\text{.}\) You do not need to come up with an explicit formula for your functions.
